# SOUL Rolls

`+roll <skill>` — Start a roll at Standard difficulty.

`+roll <skill>=<difficulty>` — Start at a configured difficulty.

`+roll/gm <skill>[=<difficulty>]` — Request GM assistance in the current scene.

When your roll is awaiting your selection:

`+roll suggested` — Accept all suggested optional Boons and Banes.

`+roll <tag> [<tag> ...]` — Choose specific owned entries.

`+roll none` — Decline optional entries. GM-mandatory entries still apply.

`+roll` — Show your current pending-roll status.

`+roll/pending` — List all your open pending rolls.

`+roll/history` — Show completed rolls.

`+roll/abort <roll id>=<reason>` — Abort your eligible pending roll.

Scene GMs and SOUL staff:

`+roll/review` — List rolls awaiting review in the current scene.

`+roll/review <roll id>` — View the privacy-filtered candidates.

`+roll/mark <roll id>=<mandatory tags>/<optional tags>` — Submit GM selections.

`+roll/forceabort <roll id>=<reason>` — Force-abort an open roll.
