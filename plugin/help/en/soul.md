# SOUL

SOUL is a story-first character progression system: Aspects and Skills,
optional Resonance, XP-driven advancement, Boons & Banes, Culminations, and
configurable rolls with GM-assisted review.

`+soul [character]` — View your SOUL Sheet: Aspects/Skills, Resonance, XP,
and condensed Boon/Bane summaries. Staff and authorized scene GMs may view
another character's Sheet.

`+soul/history [character]` — View your own, or an authorized character's,
Narrative History: a plain-language record of how the character has changed.

If your character has not been approved yet, see `help soul_chargen` for
choosing your Resonance, Skills, and starting Boons and Banes.

Everything above is also usable from the web portal, when your game has
installed it — see the plugin's `README.md` for setup.
