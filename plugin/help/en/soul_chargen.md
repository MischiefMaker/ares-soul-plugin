# SOUL Character Generation

Before approval, choose your Resonance, allocate Skill points, and select your
starting Boons and Banes.

`+chargen` - Review your current choices and remaining Skill points.

`+chargen/resonance <value>` - Select your Resonance.

`+chargen/skill <key>=<rating>` - Set a Skill to an absolute rating.

`+chargen/bnb <id or tag>[/<level>]=<explanation>` - Select a starting
Boon or Bane. The level defaults to `minor`.

`+chargen/drop <entry id>` - Remove one of your chargen selections.

These commands stop working after approval. Skill ratings cost one point per
rating level and cannot exceed the starting cap shown by `+chargen`.
